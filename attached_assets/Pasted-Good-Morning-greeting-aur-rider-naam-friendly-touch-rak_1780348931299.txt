5. Jo Remove Ya Simplify Karo
Multiple warning lines → single tappable completion card.

Session time, "You are Offline" text → toggle status hi kafi hai, online time chahiye toh small text "Online for 12 min" dikhao, offline mein kuch mat dikhao.

Max trips label → agar active order limit hai to sirf online hone ke baad "Active: 1/3" dikhao, offline mein hide karo.

Acceptance aur Rating empty state → completely hide until first ride complete.

"Standard" role tag → rider ke liye irrelevant hai, internal designation hai. Sirf admin ke liye rakhna hai to hata do yahan se.
