Earnings Page — Professional Redesign (Full Stack)
What & Why
The Earnings page has all the right data but presents it poorly: no chart on the main page (only in /summary), no trend vs yesterday, no clear commission breakdown, and the withdraw CTA is hard to find. The period filter tabs use bg-brand text-white which fails WCAG AA contrast on gold background. This redesign makes earnings instantly scannable and actionable.
